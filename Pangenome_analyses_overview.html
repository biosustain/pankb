Intra-species analyses(naive) of microbial genetic diversity


The below heatmap shows simple intra-species analyses (there might be more complicated analyses coming out later). Genes (column) in the heatmap were selected by the following criteria: for each gene, calculating its frequency in each phylogeny group. If one gene's frequency is > 70% in one phylogeny group but < 30% in all other phylogeny groups, it will be selected. The column (gene) of this heatmap is sorted by the phylogeny group: genes have frequency > 70% in phylogeny group A but < 30% in all others, genes have frequency > 70% in phylogeny group B but < 30% in all others, etc.
